Ingredients:
 - 3 eggs
 - 3 spoons of flour
 - 2 sweet cottage cheese packs ~200g
 - 1 non sweet cottage cheese pack ~200g

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees C.
 
 2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, eggs, cottage cheese. Stir until
    they are a consistent mass. Pour batter into prepared form.
 
 3. Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until a good color. Eat warm. Store in the fridge
